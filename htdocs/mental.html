Easing the Mind During the Pandemic

trigger warning: mention of self-h*rm (0:40-0:45)

Mental well-being is about understanding your feelings, handling them and being able to decide how we face difficult challenges. According to MentalHealth.gov, with postive mental health you'll be able to:

  • maximize your potential
  • contribute to communities
  • cope with daily challenges
  • be productive

What Can We Do?

The following are simple things we can do to maintain good mental well-being:

  • keep a routine throughout the day
  • keep in contact with friends and family
  • minimize screentime as much as possible
  • help and support others
  • have a hobby or find new activities to try
