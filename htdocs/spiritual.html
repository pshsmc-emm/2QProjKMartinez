What is Spiritual Wellness?

Spiritual well-being focuses on having a sense of purpose and reflecting on life to gain a deeper understanding of it. It's a connection to things that are larger than our own selves. While most people may automatically think of religion when it comes to spirituality, this is not the case. Spiritual well-being does not necessarily equate to religion and religious practices. Its benefits are:

  • Being content and finding purpose and meaning in life
  • Being able to overcome and grow or cope from challenges
  • Being able to figure out what truly matters in life
  • Being able to build meaningful relationships

How Can We Improve and Nourish It?

The following are simple things we can do to take care of our spiritual well-being:

  1. Do some yoga or meditate: Try this for just a couple of minutes a day to have some quiet and clear your mind after a busy day. Additionally, doing this increases self-awareness, and helps with concentration.

  2. Do some praying (to those that practice this): Doing this helps connect with a higher being and helps unlock faith. It can also help you find some direction when you're feeling lost in the sea of life.

  3. Write down your thoughts in a journal: This will help you reflect and learn more about your actions. It can help you assess your behaviours and figure out what things you prioritize in life.

  4. Find your hobbies: Do the things you enjoy doing and continue them. Who knows, maybe those hobbies of yours could result into something great in the future. It could also give you some sense of purpose and direction on what you may want to do if you haven't decided yet.

  5. Offer to help others: Contribute to a meaningful cause to you and help others in need. It helps you get to know your community better and can give you some sense of purpose in this world. Face-to-face activities wouldn't be the most suitable at this time, but contributing and donating to those that may have been affected by typhoons (like Typhoon Odette) or maybe even creating and sharing a drive of important resources for others to reference is a big help. It will give you a sense of happiness and purpose knowing that your efforts are for others and their own good too. It's two birds with one stone!
