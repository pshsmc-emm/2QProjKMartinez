Physical Health

Physical well-being focuses on the condition of the body and how to keep it at best level it can be.

Mental Health

Mental well-being focuses on facing the challenges of life and how we internalize those feelings and thoughts.

TEENS HEALTH TODAY

It's already been more than a year since quarantine started, but that doesn’t mean that everybody has fully adjusted. It's possible that everyone’s well-being has deteriorated, filled with uncertainty and anxiety. With that said, here are some pointers on how to look after your personal health specifically the physical, mental, spiritual, and social aspect.

Social Health

Social well-being focuses on maintain good social relationships within communities, which is currently still very limited.

Spiritual Health

Spiritual well-being focuses on having a sense of purpose and having a better understanding for the things around us.
